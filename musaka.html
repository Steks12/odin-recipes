Musaka

Description

This meal isnt very easy to make and you will need a balkan mom to help you.

This meal has a solid amount of protein, carbohydrates and fat, but its delicious.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Cream
  • Egg
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Pour oil into the pan to cover the bottom, add the onion (red onion) and garlic and simmer for a few minutes. Then add the minced meat and simmer until the meat changes color.
  2. Remove from the heat, add salt, pepper, vegeta, sweet red pepper and a little parsley.
  3. Peel the potatoes, cut them into slices, add salt, vegeta and pepper and mix well.
  4. Oil the bottom of the pan, arrange a row of potatoes, a row of minced meat until the mixture is used up. The last row must be potatoes.
  5. Cover the pan with foil, put in the oven for about 1 hour at about 1 hour at 200 degrees. Towards the end, pour a mixture of eggs and cream over everything and bake for a few more minutes until the potatoes turn yellow.
